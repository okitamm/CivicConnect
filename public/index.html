About CivicConnect

A smarter bridge between citizens and government.

CivicConnect is a multi-tenant government appointment platform built for the Philippines — connecting Filipino citizens with agencies like LTO, PSA, NBI, DFA, and PhilHealth through one unified, secure, and accessible system.

Each government office operates in a fully isolated environment, ensuring data privacy across agencies while giving system administrators full platform-level visibility and control.

Platform Features

Everything a modern civic platform needs.

Designed from the ground up for the Philippine government context — trustworthy, accessible, and built for scale.

Real-Time Appointment Booking
Citizens can browse available services, select a date and time slot, and receive instant confirmation — with live slot availability updates per office.
Multi-Tenant Isolation
Each government office operates in a fully isolated environment. LTO staff cannot see PSA records — and vice versa. Data sovereignty is enforced at the architecture level.
Staff Operations Dashboard
Branch staff get a dedicated console showing today's queue, citizen details, service type, and one-click status updates — Complete or No-Show — per appointment.
Superadmin Control Panel
The platform owner can register new government agencies, generate isolated admin credentials, and monitor system health and booking volumes across all tenants.
Slot & Queue Management
Office admins configure available time slots per service, set slot capacities, and manage walk-in queues alongside pre-booked appointments for a seamless day-of flow.
SMS & Notification Ready
Automated confirmation and reminder notifications can be configured per agency — reducing no-show rates and keeping citizens informed every step of the process.
